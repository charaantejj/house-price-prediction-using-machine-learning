# Livest - Premium AI Property Valuation

Livest is a high-end real estate intelligence platform designed for the South Indian market (Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Kochi). It utilizes advanced AI modeling to provide projected property valuations for the year 2026, helping investors and homeowners make data-driven decisions.

- **2026 Price Forecasting**: AI-driven projections based on current market trends, inflation, and infrastructure development.
- **Lifestyle Match Index**: Proprietary scoring for Connectivity, Education, and Work-Life balance.
- **Dynamic Property Discovery**: Staggered grid view of comparable high-yield investment properties.
- **Financial Tools**: Built-in Loan EMI Estimator and ROI analysis.
- **Premium UI/UX**: Ultra-modern aesthetic using glassmorphism, fluid animations (Framer Motion), and a mobile-responsive design.
- **Locality Intelligence**: Deep data on IT corridors and high-demand zones like Tellapur, Whitefield, and HITEC City.
